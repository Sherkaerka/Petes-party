Time left of the party: 10 hours

How the game works:

Pete's Party is an interactive story where you make the decisions for Pete and help him solve challenges along his way. You'll set his fate.
There will be a story part that will change the chapters depending on you choices. Each chapter is also linked to either a challenge or a choice that has to be made.
As you can see you have 10 hours until the party is over.

The story begins...

Pete’s had a rough day and now, after meeting with the principal, he’s almost too late out of school to be able to make it to his best friend’s party.
But maybe if he gets some help along the way he can make it.
How it ends is up to you...
